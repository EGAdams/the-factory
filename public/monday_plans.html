Steps for Digi Update #2

Step Explanation
1 Instead of flashing all the winner lamps at once for about 6 seconds, we will try doing a cascade of lamps from top to bottom.

4 We are not sure how long each lamp will be on for, so we will have to experiment.

6 For Digi Tennis, we will have 6 lamps flashing, each one for 200 milliseconds, and we will do this 5 times.
